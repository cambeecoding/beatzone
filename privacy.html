Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how Luis Barreto ("I", "me" or "my") handles information in connection with the BeatZone app for iPhone and this website. I take your privacy seriously and have designed BeatZone to collect as little information as possible.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact me at beatzone_support@icloud.com.

1. Who is responsible for your data

The data controller for BeatZone is Luis Barreto, based in the United Kingdom. For the purposes of UK data protection law, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, I am the controller of any personal data processed in connection with BeatZone.

2. Data collected by the BeatZone app

BeatZone does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server. All data used by the app remains on your device at all times.

The app accesses the following information solely to provide its core function:

  • Heart rate data — read from Apple HealthKit or a connected Bluetooth heart rate monitor during an exercise session. This data is used only to display your current heart rate and calculate your session average. It is never transmitted off your device and is not written back to Apple Health.
  • Heart rate zone settings — your minimum and maximum heart rate preferences are saved locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

BeatZone does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting services, or any third-party SDKs that collect personal data.

3. HealthKit

BeatZone requests permission to read heart rate data from Apple Health. This permission is entirely optional — you may decline it and use a Bluetooth heart rate monitor instead. BeatZone does not request permission to write to Apple Health, and does not share HealthKit data with any third party. Use of HealthKit data is governed by Apple's HealthKit terms, which prohibit use of HealthKit data for advertising or sale to data brokers.

4. Bluetooth

BeatZone uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to connect to compatible heart rate monitors. Bluetooth connection data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored beyond the active session.

5. This website

This support website is hosted via GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server access logs (such as IP addresses) as part of hosting the site. I do not use any analytics tools, tracking scripts, or cookies on this website. For information about GitHub's data practices, please refer to GitHub's privacy policy.

6. Support communications

If you contact me at beatzone_support@icloud.com, I will use the information you provide solely to respond to your query. I will not use it for any other purpose or share it with any third party.

7. Children

BeatZone is rated 13+ on the App Store. I do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data in connection with BeatZone, please contact me at beatzone_support@icloud.com and I will take appropriate steps.

8. Your rights under UK data protection law

Under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights in relation to any personal data I hold about you:

  • The right to be informed about how your personal data is used — which this policy provides.
  • The right of access to any personal data I hold about you.
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate personal data.
  • The right to erasure ("the right to be forgotten") in certain circumstances.
  • The right to restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • The right to data portability in certain circumstances.
  • The right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Given that BeatZone does not transmit or store personal data on any server, the personal data I hold about you is limited to any information you provide when contacting me for support. To exercise any of the rights above, please contact me at beatzone_support@icloud.com.

9. Complaints

If you have a complaint about how I have handled your personal data, please contact me in the first instance at beatzone_support@icloud.com and I will endeavour to address your concern promptly.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), which is the supervisory authority for data protection in the United Kingdom. You can do so at ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint.

10. Changes to this policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. I encourage you to review this policy periodically.
