BeatZone

Heart rate zone trainer for iPhone

BeatZone keeps you in your target heart rate zone during exercise. It has real-time visual feedback and hands-free audio announcements so you can focus on your workout.

Set your minimum and maximum heart rate, start your session, and BeatZone does the rest. Your current BPM is displayed as well as your session average.

Features

  • Real-time heart rate display with zone colour coding
  • Audio announcements of current HR and session average. It works with screen off
  • Customisable target heart rate zone
  • Session average tracking
  • Zone breach alerts when you go above or below your range
  • 30-second or 60-second announcement intervals
  • Connects via Bluetooth to chest straps and BLE heart rate monitors
  • Supports Garmin watches with BLE (enable Broadcast in your activity)
  • Supports Apple Watch via Apple Health

Compatibility

  • Requires iPhone with iOS 18 or later
  • Compatible with any Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) heart rate monitor
  • Compatible with Garmin watches and Apple Watch
